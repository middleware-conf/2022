Accommodation Information

CONFERENCE HOTEL

The 23rd ACM/IFIP International Middleware Conference has arranged special prices for accommodation. The Fairmont Le Château Frontenac which is the conference venue has special offers from November 3rd to 15th, 2022 for ACM MIDDLEWARE participants. To make the reservation, visit this page or call 1.800.441.141 by mentioning ACM MIDDLEWARE 2022.

  • 5-star hotel
  • https://www.fairmont.com/frontenac-quebec/
  • Standard room rates starting at 219 CAD per night
  • Tax is not included in the price
