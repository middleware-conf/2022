CONFERENCE REGISTRATION

REGISTRATION

Authors should register their paper by October 31st. If the registration is not received in time, the paper will not be included in the proceedings. There should be at least one full registration per paper. Students (as non authors or as additional authors) can benefit from a discount. Discounts are also available for ACM or IFIP members.

Note that the early registration for the conference ends October 1st.

To register for the conference, please click here.

WHAT IS INCLUDED IN THE REGISTRATION

Full conference registrations include participation in the main Middleware program and pre-conference program. Coffee breaks and lunches are provided, as well as one reception and one banquet dinner.

CANCELLATION POLICY

Note that no cancellation and no refunds are possible.
